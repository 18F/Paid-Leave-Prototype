How does this work?

  1. Fill out and submit this form.
  2. We’ll email you a private URL to share with your healthcare provider.
  3. Your healthcare provider will log in and provide information relevant to your claim.
  4. We process your application. You'll receive an email when we receive the package, and once it's been processed. (The average claims-processing time is currently 18 days.)
  5. You'll receive your benefits.

If you have any questions, don't hesistate to contact us. You can also read eligibility information, estimate the benefits for which you qualify, and request a paper form.
