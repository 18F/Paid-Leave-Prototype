How it works

A claim for benefits will typically involve the participation of four parties: the state department, the person claiming benefits — that’s probably you — your employer, and your healthcare provider.

  1. Fill out and submit this form.
  2. We’ll send along a code to give to your healthcare provider. Using this code, your healthcare provider will provide information relevant to your claim.
  3. We’ll process your application. The average claims-processing time is currently 18 days.
  4. You’ll receive your benefits.

If you have any questions, don’t hesistate to contact us. You can also read eligibility information, estimate the benefits for which you qualify, and request a paper form.
