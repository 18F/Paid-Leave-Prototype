How it works

Most claims for PFML benefits follow a similar process:

  1. Claimants fill out and submit the "file a claim" form.
  2. We provide claimants with a six-character code to give to their healthcare provider. Healthcare providers sign in with this information and smit information relevant to the claimant’s claim.
  3. We process the application. The average claims-processing time is currently 18 days.
  4. The claimant receives benefits.

If you have any questions, don’t hesistate to contact us.
