Paid Family Medical Leave

Under the Family Leave Insurance provision of the [State name] Temporary Disability Benefits Law, cash benefits may be payable for up to six (6) weeks to:

  • Bond with a newborn or newly adopted child, or to
  • Care for a seriously ill family member

Most workers who take family leave within 14 days of their last day of work in covered [State name] employment are covered for family leave insurance under the State Plan or an approved Private Plan. If you are not sure of your coverage you should ask your employer.

If your family leave begins more than 14 days after your last day of work in covered [State name] employment, your claim will be forwarded to our Family Leave During Unemployment section.
